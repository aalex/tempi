[DESCRIPTION]
Forwards OSC messages received at given ports to some given hostnames and ports.

[SYNOPSIS]
osc-forward [options]

[EXAMPLES]
Forward OSC message from port 10000 to port 20000 on localhost:

  osc-forward -r 10000 -s localhost:20000
